TREAT Sponsors and Partners

TREAT is grateful for the support and partnership of many organisations who were instrumental in the organisations development up until present day. This includes The Other Organisation which supported a 3 year project that addresed the rights and issues of the trans and gender non-conforming population of Zimbabwe.

This also includes OSISA which funded access to medical services for members of the trans community. OSISA also has supported the "free to be me" project that focuses on trans diverse persons knowing and accepting themselves and others amongst many other project goals.

The European Union is currently supporting the Out and Proud project which is intended to have a long term impact on creating a more favorable environment for the recognition, respect and enjoyment of human rights by LGBTI persons in Southern Africa.
