Meet our board

Hover over the pictures to view the profiles

Ricky E Nathanson

TREAT Founder

Former Executive Director of TREAT [ex officio] Ricky holds a qualification with the Institute of Chartered Secretaries & Administrators of Zimbabwe, she comes with experience in the corporate governance sector spanning twenty years. She is a leading and works international trans activism. Ricky also comes in an advisory capacity to ensure maintenance and growth of some of the existing relations she was nurturing at the time of her resignation.

Tanaka Muganyi

Board Chairman

Principal/Managing Partner of Tanaka Law Chambers Mr. Muganyi is a leading human rights lawyer in Zimbabwe, and was counsel that got the case against the Executive Director, Ms. Nathanson, acquitted in 2014, after she was unlawfully arrested for using the female bathroom at a local hotel. He still works extensively in the human rights field and proven invaluable by virtue of the skills and assistance he has brought to the organization. He single handedly got TREAT registered in 2017, the first trans rights organization to do so in Zimbabwe.

Dr. Nigel Dzvanga

Medical Practitioner

• MBChB (Zim)
Dr. Dzvanga was appointed upon his interest in pursuing a career in endocrinology and reconstructive surgery, after a sensitization training held by TREAT in 2017. He is currently back at the University of Zimbabwe where he is pursuing his Master’s degree in Endocrinology.

Pugen

Board Member

Holds Bachelor of Arts Communications. Pugen was selected as a Board Member, as he is member of the Trans diverse community. He comes with a wealth of experience in communications and media advocacy.
